About Us

Water is a vital component to all aspects on Earth, without it there would be no living species, no life, not even this planet we are on! ⅔ of Earth is covered in water, however, only 0.002% of that is freshwater and with climate change and global warming, this amount continues to decrease. Melting glaciers, changing precipitation patterns, and unpredictable river flows are just a few of the extreme weather changes that amplify negative effects on our water sources.

Canada is considered to be a freshwater-rich country with over 20% of the world's supply of freshwater. Unfortunately, Canadians are also one of largest per capita users of freshwater in the world with a domestic water consumption rate of 29,100 litres per person. To put this in perspective, that's roughly 58,200 plastic water bottles worth of water.

Here at WaterConnect, we acknowledge the freshwater crisis and strive to shed light on ways to take advantage of greywater as a way to reuse freshwater. Our team hopes to educate people on greywater as well as provide a product + app that helps greywater system owners and homeowners
