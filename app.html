Track weekly water usage

Get weekly metrics on total household water consumption versus ideal water consumption tailored to your household profile

Personalized Metrics

Based on your profile information, the WaterConnect algorithm provides personal metrics on ideal water consumption and average water use from individual water appliances

Connecting all water appliances

With the WaterConnect app, you are able to manage all your water appliances in one place.

Manage Individual Appliances

View metrics for individual appliances such as daily use and leakage. Add new appliances using the QR code provided on product to sync with app.

Get Real Time Notifications

If household has a greywater system, app can send users real time notifications for maintenance/problems in different parts of the machine based on calculations from the algorithm
